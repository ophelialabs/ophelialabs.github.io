## Cloud Sec
Purebred, FedRAMP, and FIPS form a critical ecosystem for ensuring that identities, cloud services and encryption meet mandatory US gov standards.
- Purebred: A Defense Information Systems Agency (DISA) program that provides a secure method for issuing PKI (Public Key Infrastructure) certificates to mobile devices. It allows DoD users to use derived credentials for secure email, web access, and digital signatures without requiring a physical CAC
- FedRAMP: The Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program
